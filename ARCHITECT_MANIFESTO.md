# **ARCHITECT MANIFESTO: THE SOVEREIGN ARCHITECTURE & NOIR COHESION**

## **I. EXECUTIVE PHILOSOPHY: THE INDIE-PARADOX**

We are building a system that achieves "AAA" psychological weight through "Indie" architectural minimalism. Our goal is a **Sovereign Interface**—a tactical, brutalist dashboard that feels like high-stakes institutional software.

*   **Environmental Gravity**: We do not use "gamey" tropes. Power is shown through the weight of the office: the vibrating glass, the cold-green lamp, the silence of a "Red Phone".
*   **Precision as Narrative**: High-resolution metrics like **Bond Yields**, **Unemployment**, and **Sector-Specific Stock Indices** are not just numbers; they are the "texture" of the world's stability.

---

## **II. TECHNICAL DOCTRINE: FUNCTIONAL IMMUTABILITY**

To prevent "architectural drift" and state-spaghetti, the engine is governed by the **Functional Pivot**.

1.  **Immutable State Snapshotting**: Every turn or action must produce an entirely new `State` dataclass. This enables our "Time Travel" (Undo/Redo) persistence stack.
2.  **The "Sovereign Bridge" (FastAPI)**: The backend is a pure-function engine exposed via a typed API. This ensures the React/TypeScript frontend remains a "Thin Client" focused purely on visual fidelity.
3.  **Gaussian Stochasticity**: Outcomes are never binary. We use Gaussian distributions to ensure "organic" fractional shifts (e.g., -2.34% vs. a round -5%).
4.  **Chaos Engineering**: The **Chaos Modifier** (formerly Transactional Disrupter) is a permanent +15.0% variance floor, ensuring the world never feels "safe" or predictable.

---

## **III. AESTHETIC VETTING: THE FINCHER-NOIR BASELINE**

The visual identity is non-negotiable and must be enforced at every UI commit.

*   **Chiaroscuro GUI**: The interface must utilize deep blacks, cold slate-grays, and sharp amber highlights.
*   **Tactical Map Projection**: The map is a vector-based "War Room" display, not a topographical map. It reflects **Influence**, not geography.
*   **Weighted Motion**: UI elements must have physical "heft." Transitions must be slow and deliberate (0.4s - 0.6s) to reflect the gravity of the decisions being made.

---

## **IV. COGNITIVE FRONTIER & LIMITATIONS**

We recognize that as complexity grows, our own cognitive biases or model limitations may create friction.

*   **The Best-Guess Protocol**: When implementing non-deterministic diplomacy, we label it `[EXPLORATION_BRANCH]`.
*   **Humble Refactoring**: If the state depth causes latency, we do not "hack" the solution. We pause, report to Management, and flatten the architecture.
